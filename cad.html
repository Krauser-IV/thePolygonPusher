RC Gadgetz Decal Design

I was contracted by a hobbyist RC company to design decals and sample renders for their new line of Pulse RC plane. I was also tasked with developing stencils to be used for painting the decals onto the plane models. This required me to use a hybrid workflow where I used Fusion 360 to get the models ready for retopology and painting in blender then use Photoshop and Meshmixer's Unwrapping tools to create 2D stencils that I further edited in Adobe Illustrator.

Fusion 360 CAD bike

I was contracted to design a CAD model by Eride Africa to create a CAD model of their solar bike from the reference they provided in Fusion 360 for presentation purposes. One of the more technically complex CAD proects I've gotten to work on but it was a welcome challenge.

Oppo X 2021 Product animation

I have to admit the rollable phone Oppo is working on blew my mind and with Eevee making rendering animations as accessible as ever I threw together this animation to try out the workflow between blender and Davinci Resolve.
